# Project_Spending_Analysis

## Project Description

Extracting my spendings every months to analyze them and adapting my futures budgects 

## Project Goal

Analyzing my spendings in order to save money to achieve my goal -> Buying my home

## Key Questions

- How is the repartition of my spending ?
- How is the repartition of my spending compared to lasts years ?
- How should i save money ?
- Is there a periode where i spend the most ?
- Is there a weekday where i spend the most ?
